### What is it?

Done Today is a minimalist daily progress logger designed to help you focus on what really matters. Instead of managing complex tasks or overwhelming habit lists, you capture the one thing that made your day meaningful.

It focuses on simplicity, reflection, and building a visible record of your effort without the pressure of traditional productivity systems.

### Features

- **Daily Reflection**: Log one meaningful action that moved you forward.
- **Momentum Tracking**: See your consistency build up over time.
- **Private by Default**: Your data stays on your device (browser/app).
- **Markdown Support**: Use simple markdown to style your daily entries.
- **Offline First**: Works without an internet connection.
- **Minimalist UI**: Clean, distraction-free interface built for speed.
